   - State is stored as a tuple `(current_tokens, last_timestamp)` per user.
   - If the state is `None` (first event of a user), the bucket is initialized to the full capacity of `10.0` tokens.
   - For subsequent events, refilled tokens are calculated as `elapsed_time * 2.0` and capped at `10.0` tokens.
   - Supports a special `cost` of `-1` which resets the user's bucket to full capacity and is always allowed.
   - If the bucket has enough tokens for the requested `cost`, they are deducted and `allowed: true` is returned. Otherwise, tokens are not deducted and `allowed: false` is returned.
