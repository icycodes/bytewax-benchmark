## How it works

The dataflow (`jsonl_router`) uses a cascade of three `op.branch` operators to split the stream:

1. **`route_error`** — Splits into `errors` (trues) and non-errors (falses)
2. **`route_metric`** — From non-errors, splits into `metrics` (trues) and non-metrics (falses)
3. **`route_log`** — From non-metrics, splits into `logs` (trues) and `dead_letters` (falses)

Each branch is then serialized back to JSON strings, wrapped as `(key, value)` tuples (required by `FixedPartitionedSink`), and written to its respective output file via `FileSink`.
