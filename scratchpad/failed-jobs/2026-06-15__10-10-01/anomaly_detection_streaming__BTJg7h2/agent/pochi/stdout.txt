## What was built

A stateful stream processing pipeline that:

1. **Reads** sensor readings from `data.jsonl` using a `FixedPartitionedSource` with a `StatefulSourcePartition` — the resume state (line index) is fully picklable for SQLite-based recovery.

2. **Groups** data by `sensor_id` using `op.key_on` (keys are strings as required by Bytewax).

3. **Applies a sliding window** via `fold_window` with:
   - `EventClock` using the `timestamp` field from each reading
   - `SlidingWindower` with 60s length and 20s offset (step)
   - A `WindowState` accumulator (fully picklable dataclass-like object) that stores all readings in the window

4. **Detects outliers** by computing mean and standard deviation per window, then flagging any reading where `value > mean + 3*std_dev` or `value < mean - 3*std_dev`. When `std_dev == 0`, no outliers are emitted.

5. **Outputs** anomalies to `anomalies.jsonl` in the required format.
